The railway station of Sint-Joris-Weert is one and a half kilometers away from the campsite, for those with limited luggage this is an ideal way to get to Fri3d Camp.

The campsite is easily accessible by car and we foresee enough parkingspace. But the road leading to the campsite is rather narrow and we would like to avoid needless commotion. For that reason we ask wherever possible to share a car, during Fri3d Camp we will make sure there is no unneeded car traffic on the campsite, not only to keep the great experience but also to ensure safety.
