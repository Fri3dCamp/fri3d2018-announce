Our call for participation has ended

The deadline for proposals was February 28th. Since that date has passed, it is no longer possible to submit proposals for workshops, talks or other activities.

How to get your activity at Fri3d Camp still

You are free to organise ad-hoc activities during Fri3d Camp. They will not be included in the 'official' program, but we will enable you to promote your activity during camp.

Want to be sure of a ticket? Keep a close watch on the start of ticket sales. For previous editions' participants this will start on May 1st, for everybody else ticket sales will start on May 14th. We know from experience that tickets sell out fast, so make sure you are at your computer on this date.
